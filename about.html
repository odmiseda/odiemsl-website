About Odiems Limited

Founded in 2020 by Denis Miseda in Nairobi, Odiems Limited bridges the gap between Kenya’s rich agricultural heritage and modern commerce. We source premium unrefined brown sugar directly from local mills such as Mumias, Nzoia, and Sony Sugar, ensuring fair trade and sustainable livelihoods for over 500 smallholder farmers in Western Kenya.

Our mission is simple: **empower farmers, deliver quality, and entertain responsibly**. We provide free agricultural extension through this website, wholesale sugar at competitive prices, and data‑driven SportPesa jackpot analyses that help Kenyans make informed entertainment choices.

Our Values

  • Sustainability: We promote climate‑smart farming and reduce middlemen.
  • Transparency: Every sack of sugar is traceable to its mill.
  • Community: 5% of profits fund farmer training programs.
  • Responsible Entertainment: All jackpot tips include BCLB‑compliant disclaimers.

With a growing team of agronomists, traders, and data analysts, Odiems Limited is becoming the trusted name in Kenyan agribusiness and sports insights. Join us today – whether you're a farmer, retailer, or jackpot enthusiast.
